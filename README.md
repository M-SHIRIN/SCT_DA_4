#  Business Insights Report (EDA) – Bank Marketing Campaign

##  Overview

This project presents an **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** of the **Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset** as part of **Task 04** during my **Data Analyst Internship at SkillCraft Technology**.

The primary objective was to analyze customer demographics and marketing campaign data to uncover actionable business insights that improve marketing effectiveness and increase deposit subscription rates. The accompanying report summarizes the findings and recommendations. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

##  Objectives

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
- Explore customer demographics
- Analyze marketing campaign performance
- Identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions
- Generate business insights through visualizations
- Provide data-driven recommendations

##  Key Findings

- Customers aged **30–50** were more likely to subscribe.
- Higher account balances were associated with better subscription rates.
- Certain occupations achieved stronger conversion rates.
- Contact method significantly influenced campaign success.
- Customer demographics played an important role in marketing outcomes. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Focus campaigns on high-converting customer segments.
- Prioritize communication channels with better performance.
- Personalize marketing based on customer profiles.
- Improve customer segmentation strategies.
- Continuously monitor campaign effectiveness using data-driven insights. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
